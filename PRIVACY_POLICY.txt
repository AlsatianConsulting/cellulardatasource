Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 13, 2026
App: Cellular Datasource
Package: dev.alsatianconsulting.cellulardatasource
Developer: Alsatian Consulting

Cellular Datasource is a utility app that collects cellular and GPS telemetry from your Android device and streams it to destinations you configure (for example, local host tools such as Kismet via USB/ADB forwarding).

1. Information We Process
Depending on enabled features and device capabilities, the app may process:
- Precise location data (GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, heading, accuracy)
- Cellular telemetry (for example MCC/MNC, cell ID, TAC/LAC, PCI, signal/radio measurements)
- Device/stream status data (timestamps, connection and service health)

2. How We Use Information
We use this information only to:
- Provide live telemetry collection and display in the app
- Stream telemetry to endpoints you configure
- Support app reliability (foreground service, reconnect/restart behavior)

3. Data Sharing
- We do not operate a cloud backend that receives your telemetry by default.
- Data is transmitted only to endpoints you configure.
- We do not sell personal data.

4. Data Retention
- The app is designed for live streaming and operational use.
- Persistent storage/retention is primarily controlled by downstream systems you use (for example host logs and exported files).
- You control deletion by removing those downstream logs/files and uninstalling the app.

5. Permissions
The app may request:
- ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION
- READ_PHONE_STATE
- FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION
- RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
- INTERNET
- Bluetooth permissions where applicable

These permissions are used only for enabled telemetry and app operation features.

6. Security
We recommend using trusted devices, secured host environments, and protected storage for any logs/exports. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13.

8. Your Choices
You can:
- Stop telemetry streaming at any time in the app
- Disable permissions in Android settings
- Uninstall the app

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a new Effective Date.
